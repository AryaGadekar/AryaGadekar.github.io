About Me

Hi, I’m Arya.

I analyse data for a living, but what I really do is find clarity in chaos. Most people see spreadsheets and dashboards. I see stories. Data is human behaviour translated into numbers, and every trend has a reason. Over time, curiosity turned into SQL queries and Power BI reports — my way of finding honest answers.

I love diving into messy datasets and building dashboards that make people say they finally get it. Give me unclear requirements, scattered data, and a tight deadline, and I’ll make something useful, usually with coffee and a playlist. Outside work, I travel, read, and collect small moments that change how I think. If you want to know more, the experience section has the details.
